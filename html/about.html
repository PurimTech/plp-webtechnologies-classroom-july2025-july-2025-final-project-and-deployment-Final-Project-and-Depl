About StimaOne

We are a Kenyan product built to solve everyday power problems — unexpected outages, high bills, and lack of trusted technicians. We align with Kenya's Vision 2030 and the UN Sustainable Development Goals: SDG 7 (Affordable & Clean Energy) and SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities).

Our Mission

Help households and micro-businesses control energy costs while increasing access to affordable, reliable solutions.

Our Vision

Become the leading trusted energy platform in East Africa — one analysis, one repair, and one solar install at a time.

Why StimaOne

We combine technical expertise in electrical engineering with lightweight AI insights to deliver actionable, affordable results quickly.

Impact goals

  • Reduce average household electricity bills by 10% in year one for users.
  • Enable 1,000 verified fundi bookings in the first 6 months.
  • Facilitate 500 solar leads to installers in year one.
